# Resume Bullets

- Built a reproducible retail data-cleaning pipeline on the UCI Online Retail II dataset, standardizing 525k+ transaction rows into 400k+ analysis-ready records with an auditable rejection trail.
- Rejected 124k+ problematic rows using business rules for cancellations, missing customer IDs, duplicate lines, and invalid quantity or price values.
- Produced a compact SQLite audit mart plus generated proof images and summary metrics for portfolio review and interview walkthroughs.
- Documented the cleaning methodology and business impact with real metrics rather than synthetic examples.
